AWARD WINNING INDIAN RESTAURANT IN NEW DELHI

Atulya on commercial drive in New Delhi is an award winning Indian restaurant serving truly traditional spicy aromatic curries, Indian breads, tandoori meats and vegetables carefully crafted following recipes from the states of Rajasthan, Punjab, West Bengal, Karnataka, Kerala, Chennai and Uttar Pradesh.

Atulya Commercial Drive is where you go to enjoy great food (all kinds: Ethiopian, Italian, Ecuadorian, Indian). It’s where you catch up with friends, hang out with family, shop, buy groceries, see a show, support a Vancouver band. Commercial Drive is that neighborhood, you know, the one where you feel as though you live there even if you don’t.

Relaxing indian inspired garden style seating

RESTAURANT SPACE

Atulya Restaurant is just like the Drive, it’s your place to unwind and decompress.If you’re heading North, it’s before Venables, a few doors up from Joe’s Cafe, just across the street from BierCraft. The atmosphere is light and airy, there’s a rock bed with a small water feature to ensure a calm vibe. The sound of people chatting mixed in with Indian music loud enough to dance to yet quiet enough to talk over. The staff, sounds from the kitchen, the smell of vegetables, meats, breads, and cheeses as they’re cooked with endless spice combinations. It’s all of these things that makes Sula Commercial Drive feel like the kind of place where you feel as though you’ve been here a thousand times before even if it’s just your first visit.

OUR FOOD MENU

It will be what you order, whether it’s a classic like Butter Chicken, Saag Paneer, Garlic Naan, or something different like Rajastani lamb masala, fish malabari, or pan roasted okra that will make each experience a bit different. The care that goes into making each dish, the vibe and the service will always be the same. We love what we do, the spices are roasted and ground in house, the chefs are passionate and we want people to come here for our service.
Premium cocktails & mocktails

DRINKS MENU:

Our drinks menu takes its inspiration from Indian Ingredients. We use familiar ingredients varying from spices to fruits like kokum (Garcinia Indica) & Amla (Indian Gooseberry) to team effortlessly with different spirits. At Atulya we serve refreshing cocktails including sour, fizz, muddled, highballs, straight up and classics. Draft, local, international & Indian beers, local and international wines, whiskeys from Canada, India, America, Scotland , Ireland and home made mocktails and non-alcoholic drinks.
Home delivery, take out & catering
